Project Details

For this project, I leveraged CATIA software, a powerful tool in the field of Computer-Aided Design (CAD), to craft a distinctive chess set inspired by Mechanical Engineering tools. The intricate designs were meticulously created using CATIA, drawing upon my knowledge of engineering tools and techniques.

Subsequently, the conceptualized designs were materialized into physical prototypes through the utilization of CNC (Computer Numerical Control) machines in our laboratory. This process exemplified the seamless integration of digital design with precision manufacturing methodologies, resulting in the production of high-quality, intricately detailed chess pieces.

Overall, the project not only demonstrated my proficiency in CAD software like CATIA but also showcased the synergy between digital design capabilities and advanced manufacturing technologies, highlighting the intersection of creativity and precision in engineering endeavors.
