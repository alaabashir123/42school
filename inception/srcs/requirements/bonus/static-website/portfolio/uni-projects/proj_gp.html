Project Details

My graduation project revolved around the design and development of a cutting-edge cooling system aimed at optimizing heat transfer efficiency through the utilization of novel cooling media. The project focused on harnessing advanced heat transfer principles and innovative cooling techniques to create a highly efficient and sustainable cooling solution for various applications.

The project involved extensive research into novel cooling media with superior heat transfer properties, including advanced fluids, phase-change materials, and material with high thermal conductivity. Through experimentation and analysis, the project aimed to identify the most effective cooling media for improving heat dissipation and thermal management in diverse environments.

By undertaking this project, I acquired invaluable experience in thermal engineering, heat transfer analysis, and innovative system design. The project not only expanded my technical knowledge but also equipped me with the skills to address complex engineering challenges and develop sustainable solutions with real-world impact.
